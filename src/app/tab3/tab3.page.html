For decades, we've been dedicated to gathering and sharing details about the most exciting automotive events in the area. Our journey began with flyers, but as technology advanced, so did we. We moved on to Hemmings event finder and then created a sub-site on the Kansas City Automotive Museum website. Today, we are proud to offer our app, available on both Apple and Android devices.
